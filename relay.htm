The Relay

A relay is an electromechanical switch that makes use of an inductor coil to move the switching contact.

The symbol for an SPDT relay looks like this:

Relays act like transistors, in that they have a switching portion and a control portion.

You can use a relay with a transistor, but be sure to use a flyback diode to protect your transistor!

Relays can be used on AC or DC, depending on which relay you get. AC relays are used in appliances, and DC relays are used in automotive, etc.
