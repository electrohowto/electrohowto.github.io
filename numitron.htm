The Numitron

A Numitron is a display device that uses incandescent filaments as segments.

The Numitron comes in a tube, and usually has seven segments. Here is a real-life example:

Some old equipment of the 1970s would use these tubes to display information.

To use a Numitron tube, place 3V on the common pin, and ground a segment. You can even switch the polarity, or run them off an AC square wave (like an LCD), since these tubes are essentially lamps!
